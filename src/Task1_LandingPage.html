City of Joy

Kolkata

Immerse yourself in the vibrant culture of Kolkata. Explore historic landmarks, savor delectable street food, and witness the charm of the Howrah Bridge. Your journey begins in the City of Joy!

Gateway to the South

Chennai

Chennai welcomes you with its rich history and modern vibrancy. From the serene Marina Beach to the bustling streets of Mylapore, discover the cultural tapestry of Southern India in this coastal metropolis.

God's Own Country

Kerala

Embark on a journey through the enchanting backwaters of Kerala. Cruise on traditional houseboats, indulge in aromatic spices, and witness the lush landscapes that earned Kerala its heavenly nickname.

Paradise on Earth

Kashmir

Lose yourself in the picturesque valleys and snow-capped peaks of Kashmir. From the serene Dal Lake to the vibrant markets of Srinagar, experience the magic that has made Kashmir a timeless destination.
